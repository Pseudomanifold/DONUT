This is a database of applications of Topological Data Analysis, an emerging mathematical paradigm for performing multi-scale analyses of complex data sets.

Where is the data coming from?

The source for all articles in this database is a bibliographical database maintained by Barbara Giunti, and created in 2020 together with JƒÅnis Lazovskis. This database is currently accessible via Zotero. This service indexes the database daily to provide a full-text search over individual articles, authors, and tags.

How can I report erroneous data?

To report an error in an article, please contact bgiunti@tugraz.at. We are working on providing a direct feedback opportunity for database articles as well.

Why is my article missing?

To report a missing article, please contact bgiunti@tugraz.at. It is easiest to provide a BibTeX entry of your article to the inclusion into the database.

Who maintains this database frontend?

This frontend was developed, hosted, and maintained by Bastian Rieck. It is powered by Xapian, Flask, and Debian.
